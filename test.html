SILUNI (Sistem Alumni Universitas Trunojoyo Madura)

SILUNI is a web-based application developed to manage and organize alumni data from Universitas Trunojoyo Madura. The primary goal of SILUNI is to streamline the alumni database, making it easier for both the university administration and alumni network to stay connected and up-to-date. This platform provides various features to facilitate efficient data management, enhance communication, and support alumni engagement.
